fire Strategy

Brand strategy is the basis of every successful company. Only through an intensive examination of the target group, the existing market and your own company is it guaranteed that a company becomes a brand that stands out positively and that people are fans and customers*transformed inside.

fire design

For brand design (corporate design), the developed brand strategy is translated into logo, fonts, typography, design and visual language, etc. In this way, the brand identity is actually visually visible and tangible. Only through strategic design is it guaranteed that the right customers are addressed and that your company stands out from the mass of competitors.

Visual identity

The visual identity is part of the brand design. It includes all visual elements such as logo, colors, fonts and graphic elements.

web design

A company's website is their online flagship. It is usually the first medium that potential customers see from a company and therefore a significant part of your brand. In order to create a holistic brand experience, it is important that the brand identity is also recognizable in the design of the website.

web Development

Functionality and performance of the website are just as important as design and aesthetics. Data protection regulations and DSGVO must also be observed. Depending on your needs, we implement your website with Wordpress and Elementor, Squarespace or Custom Code.

Collateral

Collateral are all contact points between a brand and its customers. These include e.g. the design website, social media templates, flyers, business cards, packaging or shop interior. In order to make your brand tangible for customers in all contact points, it is important that the brand design is applied uniformly on all channels.

Branded spaces

Branded spaces or corporate architecture expands the core brand values and properties into the third dimension, so that we can experience the brand identity in interiors and buildings. This can e.g. in retail & shops, Office or practice rooms, restaurants & cafes, showrooms or exhibitions. Because the brand is uniformly recognizable and noticeable from the logo to the interior, potential customers can better identify with the company and build an emotional bond.

fire mentoring

Do you have individual questions? Then our brand mentoring is the right thing for you. In 1: 1 online sessions you can ask us everything about positioning, branding or visibility. We share experiences and tips with you that you can implement directly.
